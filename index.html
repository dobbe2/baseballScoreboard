Welcome to Baseball Interactive Scoreboard

This is a functional representation of a scorekeeping ticker seen at the bottom of a sports cast.

Use the pitches and hits buttons to change the count (balls and strikes) and to move the runners around the bases.

Runners will continue to round the bases until there are 3 outs.

If you would like, you can choose from the list of MLB teams to set the logo. If you look carefully, there may be a few easter eggs in there!
